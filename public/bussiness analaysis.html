What is Business Analysis?

Business analysis is the process of identifying business needs and determining solutions to improve performance, increase efficiency, and support strategic decision-making.

The Business Analysis Process

Understanding Business Needs: Identify pain points, analyze data, and define business objectives.

Process Mapping & Optimization: Use BPM techniques to model and refine workflows.

Data Analysis & Decision Making: Leverage analytics to support business strategy and process improvements.

Implementation & Monitoring: Execute changes, track performance, and ensure continuous improvement.
